Sauerkraut

Gonna be honest, again, I added this to the list because it it has to be the worst food ever made.

Ingredients

  • 8 cups red or green cabbage (finely grated or chopped)
  • 1 ½ – 2 tsp sea salt (plus more to taste)
  • 1 small beet (finely shredded)
  • 3 whole carrots (finely shredded)
  • 3 Tbsp fresh ginger (shredded or grated)
  • 3 Tbsp fresh tumeric (shredded/grated)
  • 4 cloves garlic (finely minced)

Steps

  1. Add cabbage to a large mixing bowl, and top with two
    teaspoons of salt. Add beet,carrot,ginger,tumeric, and mix.
  2. Pour into a bowl and feast!
