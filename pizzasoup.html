Pizza Soup

While this may look like chili, its not! its PIZZA in a SOUP. Also, upon further research, turns out pizza soup is a real thing.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 2 cans condensed tomato soup
  • 3.5oz of Pepperoni (quartered)
  • 1 medium sweet red pepper (chopped)
  • 1 medium green pepper (choppedL
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1/2 teasopoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1-1/2 cups of mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. In a Dutch oven, bring the tomatoes, soup and water to a boil.
    Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 15 minutes. Mash with a
    potato masher. Add the pepperoni, red and green peppers,
    mushrooms, garlic, sage, basil, oregano, salt and pepper.
    Cover and simmer for 10 minutes or until vegetables are tender
  2. Ladle into ovenproof bowls. Top each with a slice of bread and
    sprinkle with cheese. Broil 4 in. from the heat until cheese is
    melted and bubbly.
