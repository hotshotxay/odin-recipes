Rock Tacos

Im going to be completely honest, I made this up when I first started the project because I thought it was funn. Its not anymore.

Ingredients

  • taco shell
  • ground beef
  • seasonings (salt, pepper, ground beef seasoning
  • lettuce
  • refried beans
  • red onions
  • shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef on stove
  2. Get all ingredients and fill taco shell
  3. Step into backyard, pick up some delicous looking rocks and add them into the taco
